# Portfolio Design Direction

> Base: my published Claude Design system **"Skyline Aerial"** (set as default).
> Reused as-is for the portfolio — monochrome and cinematic suits a personal dev showcase.
> Full token reference lives in `DESIGN.md`.

## Palette
- Primary: #161618 (near-black ink — headlines, nav, strong text)
- Accent: #0A0A0B (ink-deep — primary CTA pills & inverted blocks; *system is monochrome, so the only chromatic accent is the functional success-green #2F8F63 used on the "Available" status dot, nothing else*)
- Background: #FFFFFF (pure white; #F7F7F9 tinted for alternating sections)
- Text: #2E2E33 (body) / #5B5B63 (muted) — strong text uses #161618

## Typography
- Display (headlines): Sora (700–800, tight tracking −0.02 to −0.03em)
- Body: Inter (400–600)
- Scale: Display 64 · H1 48 · H2 36 · Lead 19 · Body 16 · Eyebrow 13 (uppercase, tracked)

## Visual language
Editorial-typographic, monochrome-cinematic. Big Sora headlines on generous whitespace.
Sections separated by single hairline rules (#E6E6EA), not heavy boxes. White cards at
16px radius with soft, low-opacity neutral shadows; dark `#0A0A0B` blocks for stats and the
footer. No gradients-as-decoration, no color for emphasis — value, weight, and scale carry it.
**One cinematic hero moment** (restrained, greyscale — subtle motion/parallax), everything
else calm and fast.

## Tone
Tagline: "I design and ship AI-powered websites — from first prototype to live URL."

## Voice
Confident-direct, technically precise. First person, short sentences, no buzzwords. States what
shipped and how it works; treats the reader as a peer, not a prospect.
